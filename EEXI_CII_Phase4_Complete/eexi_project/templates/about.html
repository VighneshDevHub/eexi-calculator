Methodology & References

How the EEXI calculation is implemented and which IMO regulations it follows.

Regulatory Basis

This calculator implements the EEXI method as defined in IMO Resolution MEPC.350(78), adopted at the 78th session of the Marine Environment Protection Committee (June 2022). EEXI became mandatory under MARPOL Annex VI Regulation 21 from 1 January 2023.

Attained EEXI Formula

The attained EEXI is calculated at 75% of the main engine's Maximum Continuous Rating (MCR):

Attained EEXI =
PME × CF,ME × SFCME + PAE × CF,AE × SFCAE

Engine Power Limitation (EPL)

When attained EEXI exceeds the required value, the vessel must implement EPL or ShaPoLi. The calculator derives the required power limit by rearranging the attained EEXI formula:

Max PME = (Req. EEXI × Capacity × Vref − PAE × CF,AE × SFCAE) ÷ (CF,ME × SFCME)
Limited MCR = Max PME ÷ 0.75
