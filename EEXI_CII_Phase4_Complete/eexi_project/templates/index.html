📋
What is EEXI?
Energy Efficiency Existing Ship Index — mandatory for ships ≥400 GT from 1 Jan 2023 (IMO MEPC.350(78)).
⚙
What is EPL?
Engine Power Limitation — limits MCR to reduce CO₂ per tonne-nm. Required if attained EEXI exceeds the required value.
📐
Formula basis
Attained EEXI = (PME × CF × SFC) / (Capacity × Vref) per MEPC.350(78) Annex 9.
