§4.1 Voyage Adjustmentsoptional

Deduct fuel & distance for safety scenarios (MARPOL Annex VI reg. 3.1) or sailing in ice conditions with an ice-classed ship.

Dx — must match voyage fuel deduction
§4.2 STS / Shuttle Correctiontankers only

For STS voyages: ≤600 nm, voyage time ≤72 h. Shuttle tankers: equipped with dynamic positioning. Formula: AFSTS = 6.1742 × DWT−0.246  |  AFShuttle = 5.6805 × DWT−0.208

§4.3 Electrical Correctionsoptional

Deduct fuel used for: reefer containers, cargo cooling/reliquefaction (gas/LNG carriers), or electric discharge pumps (tankers).

From kWh meter. Use this OR reefer-days below.
175 (2-stroke) or 200 (4-stroke) default
§4.4–4.5 Boiler & Pump Correctionstankers only

Deduct fuel consumed by oil-fired boilers for cargo heating/discharge (§4.4), and standalone engine-driven cargo discharge pumps (§4.5).

    Annual measure
    Unlike EEXI (one-time), CII is assessed every calendar year based on actual fuel consumption and distance sailed.

    Tightening targets
    Required CII reduces each year (5% in 2023 → 9% in 2025 → 11% in 2026+) making A–C ratings harder to achieve over time.

    D/E consequences
    Rating D for 3 consecutive years, or E for any year, requires SEEMP update with corrective action plan.
